Research


I am interested in using geodetic and remote sensing tools to quantify recent land motion and crust deformation and how these changes affects our environment. Datasets such as SAR provide a huge opportunity to communities and researchers that cannot perform fieldwork. Besides, the understanding of the different geological, geophysical and anthropological processes causing crust deformation are crucial to understand how to manage natural (and scarce!) resources such as groundwater.
Therefore, my main interests include remote sensing, geodesy, hydrology, and coding (the best and almost the only way to use large datasets is coding!)
